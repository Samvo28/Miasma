Miasma


Resource Gathering Survival Simulator

Main - Blog - Gallery - Roadmap

Miasma is a survival game where you need to survive inside a dome which is protected by a generator that gives energy to keep the dome powered. In the dome you will need to build machines and automate them to produce resources you need, research to unlock new technologies and farm for food to survive. You need to venture outside of the dome to find resources, lost technologies and find out how to counter the Miasma. But beware of the monsters that are lurking in the dark.

    Planned features
  • Maintain the generator by gathering crystals from the Miasma
  • Explore outside of the dome to scavenge for resources and lost technologies
  • Use machines to make different kinds of resources, gear and more machines
  • Automate your machines to improve efficiency and spend more time to exploring
  • Find DNA to clone animals and crops
  • Farm for food to help you survive the bitter darkness of the Miasma


World of Miasma

Somewhere in the future a small rock crashes in a remote part of earth, from the rock oozes a dark goo which consumes everything it touches. While it kept growing undisturbed, the organism was eventually discovered but it was futile. The goverments tried to destroy the organism as a result the organism began to spawn deformed creatures from itself. There was some progress for conventional weapons to destroy the creatures but those weapons didn't work against the organism itself. While the military was fighting against the organism, the scientists were researching it to develop countermeasures. They developed a shield which blocks to organism from passing through but the downside is that the shield needs to be powered by a part of the organism itself. Because humankind was losing the fight against the organism, domes where being build as an emergency solution. A crew just finished building a dome while the crew was celebrating, there were sounds of gunfire in the distance waiting anxiously to hear from the military creatures came from the woods. While the crew was attacked and massacred you were the only one to make it inside the safety of the dome.
